The three moves that actually compound

1. Use AI as leverage

Pick the right tools for your role and use them weekly until they're muscle memory. The people who win aren't the ones who fear AI — they're the ones who get fluent first.

2. Move up the judgment stack

As routine tasks get automated, the work that stays is judgment, taste, and ownership. Build the skills that put you closer to the decision, not the artifact.

3. Build adjacencies

Identify the adjacent role or industry where your current experience compounds — and make a deliberate move toward it before you need to.
